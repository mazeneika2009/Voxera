Frequently Asked Questions

How does the digital sizing work?

Our clothing is designed with standard international sizing. For our digital-only items, files are compatible with most major 3D avatars and metaverse platforms.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, VOID ships to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping times vary based on location, but typically range from 5-10 business days.

What is the return policy?

Physical items can be returned within 30 days of receipt if they are unworn and in original packaging. Digital assets are non-refundable once downloaded.

Are the materials sustainable?

Absolutely. We use a blend of recycled synthetics and organic cottons. Our goal is to minimize our carbon footprint while maximizing durability.
