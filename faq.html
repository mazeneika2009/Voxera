Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find my size?

We recommend referring to our detailed size guide located on each product page. Our cuts are generally true to size, but for an oversized aesthetic, we suggest sizing up.

What materials do you use?

Voxera utilizes premium, heavyweight cottons, technical nylons, and sustainable blends. Each garment is crafted for durability and comfort.

How should I care for my garments?

To ensure longevity, wash on cold with like colors and hang dry. Avoid harsh detergents and tumble drying to preserve the fabric integrity and print quality.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship globally. Orders are processed within 24-48 hours, and international delivery typically takes 5-12 business days depending on the destination.
